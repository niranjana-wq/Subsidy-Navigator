# Subsidy Navigator

## Bridging the Gap Between Government Support and Eligible Beneficiaries

Subsidy Navigator is a platform that helps farmers and small businesses find government schemes they're eligible for. It makes it easy for them to understand requirements prepare applications and track progress. All of this is done through a easy-to-use interface.

## The Problem

Governments give out a lot of money in welfare programs, subsidies, grants and incentives every year.. Many people who are eligible for these benefits do not receive them. This happens because:

- They do not know about the schemes.

- The eligibility criteria are hard to understand.

- Information is spread across government portals.

- The documentation requirements are confusing.

- The application processes are long and complicated.

- Many users rely on intermediaries or consultants.

As a result deserving beneficiaries miss out on opportunities. Also allocated funds often remain underutilized.

## Our Solution

Subsidy Navigator acts as an assistant. It simplifies the subsidy application journey.

Of searching through multiple websites and documents users provide their details once. Then they receive:

- A list of schemes they're eligible for

- Verification of their eligibility

- A list of required documents

- Guidance on how to apply

- A way to track their progress

The platform reduces complexity. It improves access to government benefits.

## How It Works

### Step 1: User Profile Creation

The user provides:

- Their location

- Their occupation

- Details about their land ownership

- Business information

- Their income category

- A list of documents

### Step 2: Scheme Discovery

The AI scans available government programs. It identifies schemes to the users profile.

**Example Output:**

- PM-KISAN

- Agricultural Equipment Subsidy

- MSME Credit Support Scheme

- Solar Pump Subsidy Program

### Step 3: Eligibility Verification

The system evaluates:

- Age requirements

- Income limits

- Geographic restrictions

- criteria

- Business registration requirements

Users receive a clear eligibility assessment for each scheme.

### Step 4: Application Assistance

The platform guides users through:

- The information

- Form completion

- Supporting documents

- Submission readiness

### Step 5: Document Verification

The system generates a personalized checklist. It identifies:

- Missing documents

- information

- Potential application issues

### Step 6: Application Tracking

Users can monitor application progress through stages such as:

- Submitted

- Under Review

- Approved

- Rejected

The platform provides updates and notifications.

## Human-in-the-Loop Approach

Subsidy Navigator assists users throughout the process.. It does not automatically submit applications.

Users remain responsible for:

- Reviewing recommendations

- Verifying information

- Uploading documents

- Confirming submissions

This ensures transparency and trust.

## Why This Matters

The challenge is not the absence of government support programs. It is the difficulty of discovering, understanding and applying for them.

Subsidy Navigator uses AI to remove these barriers. It ensures that eligible beneficiaries can access the support intended for them.

By simplifying scheme discovery and application assistance the platform helps bridge the gap between government initiatives and the people they are designed to serve.
